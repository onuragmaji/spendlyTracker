Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service to you. Specifically:

  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the Service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, data will be permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. We may use limited third-party infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting services) solely to operate the Service. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

Spendly does not integrate with any external financial institutions or payment processors.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please reach out through the contact information available on the Spendly platform. We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries promptly.
